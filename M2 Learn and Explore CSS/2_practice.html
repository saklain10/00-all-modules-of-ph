Green University of Bangladesh

Introduction

Green University of Bangladesh (GUB) is a private university located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was established in 2003 under the Private University Act 1992. GUB is known for providing quality education with a focus on ethical and sustainable practices.Its permanent campus located in the Purbachal American City, Dhaka.

Programs of GUB

Green University offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across a range of disciplines, including:

  • Engineering: Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Textile Engineering.
  • Arts and Social Sciences: English, Journalism and Media Communication (JMC), Sociology.
  • Business: Business Administration (BBA, MBA).
  • Law: LLB (Honors), LLM.

Departments

There are 9 departments in GUB, including:

  • Electrical and Electronic Engineering(EEE)
  • Computer Science and Engineering(CSE)
  • Journalism and Media Communication
  • Green Business School(GBS)
  • Software Engineering(SWE)
  • Sociology and Antropology
  • Textile Engineering(TE)
  • English
  • LAW

Green University of Bangladesh continues to grow as a reputable institution, fostering innovation and sustainability in higher education.
