My Sundarban Travel Blog

The Sundarbans is a big forest near the ocean, stretching across Bangladesh and West Bengal, India. You can get there from places like Khulna and Mongla in Bangladesh.

It’s famous because it’s really beautiful and full of nature. Imagine it like a big maze of rivers and lakes! But it’s not just pretty, it’s important for nature too.

There are special trees called mangroves and lots of animals like deer, bears, tigers, crocodiles, and monkeys living there. People think it’s so special that in 1987, it became a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Lots of people visit every year because it’s so unique and amazing.
